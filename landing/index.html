About

Pebblic Speaking is an application built in Simply.js for the Pebble to help make public speaking easy and comfortable for anyone.

Pebblic Speaking offers three services:

  • Timed practice
  • Reminders during delivery
  • Basic notecards

Timed practice lets users time intervals between topics and determine how fast they want to make the speech. Pebblic Speaking computes the average times of each topic after several practices.

The app then can pull this data to provide reminders during the delivery of the speech to encourage the user to move on to the next topic with a timely buzz.

Finally, the app can serve as basic notecards for the speaker by providing the topics one after another, accessed one at a time by either a button click or a flick of the Pebble, without a time limit or recording.





Made at PennApps, Spring 2014.
